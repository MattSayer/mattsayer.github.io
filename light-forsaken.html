Light Forsaken

Release: 31st October 2022
Genre: Horror
Engine: Unity
Platforms: Windows, Mac
Store page: Itch.io

Light Forsaken is a lo-fi horror game built around limited visibility. Armed with a sonar-like pulsing device that briefly illuminates their surroundings, players explore a small-town shopping mall and evacuate the denizens trapped inside.

With Light Forsaken, I feel that I delivered a suitably moody atmosphere, with quirky dialogue and simple-yet-effective scares.
