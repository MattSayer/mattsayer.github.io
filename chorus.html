Chorus of the Night

Release: 28th Dec 2022
Genre: Twin-stick shooter
Engine: Unity
Platforms: Windows
Store page: Steam

Chorus of the Night was my first Steam release. It’s a roguelite twin-stick shooter with a musical theme, tasking players with fighting off waves of enemies styled after elements of the music scenes of the ‘90s and early 2000s.

While there’s no shortage of roguelite wave-survival games these days, I feel the distinct theme and its incorporation into all the core gameplay elements helps Chorus of the Night stand out.
